Background

Back in 2012, I started out my post secondary education at Simon Fraser University (SFU). Like most high school gradruates I went into the experience a little lost, making my way through by taking classes that sounded interesting or that I had been good at in highschool. Eventually I stumbled onto computer science, which nurtured my interest in problem solving and critical thinking in a technical way.

I realized, as I continued pursuing my post-secondary degree, I wasn't only concerned about the efficiency of the applications that I was creating. I was also interested in creating great user experiences. I eventually found my way into an Interactive Arts and Technology (IAT) minor. IAT encouraged me to continue on with a user-centered mindset and brought my skills in front-end development to a higher level.
